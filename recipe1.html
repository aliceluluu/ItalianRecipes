INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup of pine nuts
  • 3 cups packed fresh basil leaves from about 3 oz
  • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino cheese
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

PROCEDURE

  1. Chop the pine nuts in a food processor,
  2. Cut the cherry tomatoes into 4 pieces, and remove and discard the seeds.
  3. Place them in the food processor along with garlic, basil leaves, pecorino, and olive oil until you get a rough paste.
  4. Add water to a saucepan and bring to a boil.
  5. Add the pasta and cook until al dente
  6. Drain the pasta and reserve 1/3 cup (80ml) of cooking liquid.
  7. Place the pasta in a pan with the pesto and stir.
  8. Finally, add the reserved cooking water and stir again until the sauce is creamy.
  9. Done!
  10. Serve pasta topped with extra Pecorino, basil leaves, and cherry tomatoes.
