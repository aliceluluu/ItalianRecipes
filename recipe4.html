INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups of whole or 2% milk
  • 175 g of semolina flour
  • 1 tsp of salt plus more as desired
  • 4 tblsp of unsalted butter divided, plus more for greasing
  • 1 heaping cup of freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • A large pinch of freshly grated nutmeg
  • 2 large egg yolks

PROCEDURE

  1. Start by heating the milk in a large saucepan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Then slowly pour in the semolina flour.
  3. Cook over low heat for approximately 15 to 20 minutes or until the mixture becomes very thick.
  4. Remember to stir vigorously the entire time.
  5. Now add the butter, salt, parmigiano cheese and the grated nutmeg.
  6. Finally add the two large egg yolks.
  7. Place and spread into a thin layer the mixture on a sheet of parchment in order for it to cool.
  8. Use biscuit cutters or a glass to cut the mixture into round circles.
  9. While you place them in the greased baking sheet, overlap them.
  10. When finished sprinkle more cheese and add some butter.
  11. Insert it in the oven until golden brown and bubbling (approximately 45min).
