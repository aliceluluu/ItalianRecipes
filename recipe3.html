INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 medium eggplants, cut into 1/2-inch-thick round slices
  • Salt, as needed, plus 1 tablespoon
  • 5 cups of fresh breadcrumbs
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon of dried thyme
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • All-purpose flour for dredging
  • 6 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tblsp of whole milk
  • Olive oil, as needed
  • 7 cups of marinara sauce
  • 2/3 cup of grated Parmesan, divided
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella, thinly sliced

PROCEDURE

  1. Start by arranging the eggplant slices on several baking sheets, then sprinkle them with salt.
  2. Set this aside for an hour so that the bitter juices weep from the vegetable.
  3. When this is done, rinse the eggplant using a colander under cold water.
  4. Dry it with paper towels.
  5. In a large bowl, whisk together the 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, breadcrumbs, oregano, thyme, and pepper.
  6. In a bowl, whisk the egg and milk together.
  7. Dredge an eggplant slice in the flour, then dip it in the egg, and finally dredge it in the breadcrumb mixture.
  8. Shake off any excess breading and transfer the eggplant to a baking sheet.
  9. Repeat with the remaining eggplant.
  10. In a large straight-sided skillet, pour the oil to a depth of 1/2 inch.
  11. Heat the oil over medium heat until it registers 400 degrees F on a deep frying thermometer.
  12. Now start frying the eggplant slices, turning once until golden brown. (approximately 3 minutes)
  13. Using tongs, transfer to a paper towel-lined baking sheet and season with salt to taste.
  14. Lightly brush a baking dish with olive oil, then cover it with 1/3 of the marinara sauce and arrange half of the eggplant over the sauce.
  15. Cover the eggplant with another 1/3 of the sauce. Scatter half of the Parmesan and half of the mozzarella over the sauced eggplant.
  16. Bake at 400 degrees F until hot and just beginning to brown, (about 30 minutes).
  17. Done!
  18. Serve immediately.
