INGREDIENTS :

  • 4 bread slices (preferibly pagnotta bread)
  • 1 tsp of dried oregano
  • 1 tsp of dried thyme
  • 2 tblsp olive oil, plus extra to drizzle
  • 250 g of mixed cherry tomatos tomatoes (red and yellow)
  • 2 tsp of sugar
  • 1/2 garlic clove, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup basil leaves, shredded
  • 1 cup of fresh ricotta
  • 1 tsp of salt

PROCEDURE

  1. Place the bread on a grill to medium-high and toast with olive oil,
  2. Turn the bread halfway for 3-4 minutes until golden.
  3. Meanwhile, combine tomatoes, sugar, garlic, and vinegar in a bowl.
  4. In another dish, combine fresh ricotta, 1 tsp of salt, and the dried herbs, then spread the mixture evenly over the toast.
  5. Done!
  6. To serve, divide tomato mixture among the ricotta toasts and top with basil and a drizzle of olive oil
