INGREDIENTS

  • 75g of veal (two slices)
  • 200g of Porcini Mushroom or others kind (fresh or dry)
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • few leafes of Parsley
  • 40g of butter
  • 1 of tbs flour
  • 1 tbs of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 cup of Broth or white wine

PROCEDURE

  1. Soak the dry porcini mushroom under fresh water for about 30 minutes, then dry them with paper towels.
  2. If you have fresh mushroom clean them and slice them in strips.
  3. Thinnely chop the parsley and the garlic, then prepare the vegetable broth.
  4. Season the veal lightly with salt and pepper on both sides, then cover them with flour.
  5. Make sure to remove the excess flour.
  6. Heat the pan with half butter and half oil. When the oil is hot, put the meat on the pan and cook it over high heat for 1 minute on each side.
  7. In the same pan add the rest of the oil and butter, the shredded garlic and put the fire at low heat for a few minutes.
  8. Finally, add the mushrooms.
  9. Season with salt and pepper and let it cook at medium heat for few minutes.
  10. Add the wine or broth and cook for other five minutes.
  11. To finish add a little bit of parsley.
  12. Done!
  13. Serve immediately.
